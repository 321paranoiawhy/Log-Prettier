# `base`

`Base` is from `"0"` ~ `"9"` and `"A"` ~ `"B"`.

`"0"` ~ `"9"` is corresponding to `"0"` ~ `"9"`.

`"A"` ~ `"B"` is corresponding to `"10"` ~ `"35"`.
